    About Us

    The watermill

    The old stone house has been our beloved home for the past eight years. It was built many years ago and originally served as a watermill for a villager. Nowadays, it is used as a coffee shop.

    The Waterfalls

    The water flows down from the mountain, forming three waterfalls. Two of them are located near to the watermill, while the third lies in a hidden spot, just five minutes beyond it. Ask for more details.

    Our coffee shop is housed in an old watermill, built in the early 1900s and serving the villages around us for more than half a century. Since 2018, this historic place continues its story in a new way, offering warmth, coffee, and hospitality by the Kalarritikos River. With trails leading to Kalarrites and Syrako, and waterfalls close by, it is a place where tradition, nature, and simple pleasures come together.
